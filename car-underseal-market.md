<p><p>The car underseal market consists primarily of two types: bitumen underbody seal and wax-based underbody seal. Bitumen underbody seal provides a durable, waterproof barrier that protects vehicles from rust and corrosion, making it ideal for harsh environments. In contrast, wax-based underbody seal offers a more flexible and less messy application, forming a protective layer that can self-heal small scratches and resist moisture. Both types serve to extend the lifespan of vehicles by safeguarding against environmental damage.</p></p>
