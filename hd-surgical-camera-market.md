<p><ul><li>Cmos</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HD surgical camera market features two primary types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors. CMOS cameras are known for their lower power consumption, faster image processing, and integration capabilities, offering high image quality at a competitive cost. In contrast, CCD cameras provide superior light sensitivity and image quality, making them ideal for low-light surgical environments. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing surgical visualization, enabling more precise procedures and improving patient outcomes in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
