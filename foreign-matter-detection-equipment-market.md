<p><p>The foreign matter detection equipment market is categorized into two primary types: offline and online detection equipment. In contrast, online detection equipment operates in real-time during production processes, continuously scanning products as they move along the production line. This type guarantees immediate detection of foreign materials, enhancing efficiency and product safety, ultimately reducing potential recalls and liability costs.</p></p>
