<p><p>The Integrated Circuit Manufacturing Equipment market comprises several key types essential for semiconductor fabrication. Photolithography machines create circuit patterns on silicon wafers using light, while etchers remove material to define structures. Ion implanters introduce dopants to modify electrical properties. Film deposition equipment applies thin layers of materials, and cleaning equipment ensures surfaces are free of contaminants. Testing equipment assesses the performance and reliability of circuits. Additionally, other equipment includes various support tools that contribute to the manufacturing process.</p></p>
