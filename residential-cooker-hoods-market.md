<p><p>The residential cooker hoods market is categorized primarily into three types: ducted, ductless, and convertible hoods. Ducted hoods expel air outside, providing efficient ventilation, while ductless hoods filter and recirculate air within the kitchen, making them suitable for areas lacking exterior access. Convertible hoods offer flexibility, allowing users to switch between ducted and ductless modes based on needs. Each type caters to different preferences, kitchen layouts, and ventilation requirements, influencing consumer choices in home cooking environments.</p></p>
