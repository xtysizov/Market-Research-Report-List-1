<p><p>The thrombolysis market can be categorized into three main types: surgical treatment, drug treatment, and other treatments. Surgical thrombolysis involves mechanical procedures to dissolve blood clots, often performed in emergencies. Drug treatment utilizes thrombolytic agents, such as clot-busting medications, to dissolve clots pharmacologically. Other treatments may include adjunct therapies like catheter-directed thrombolysis or the use of devices for clot removal. Each type offers distinct approaches catering to varying clinical needs in managing thromboembolic conditions.</p></p>
