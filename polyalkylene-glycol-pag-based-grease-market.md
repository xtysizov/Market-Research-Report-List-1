<p><p>The Polyalkylene Glycol (PAG)-based grease market is divided into two main types: water-insoluble and water-soluble (dissolved in water) formulations. Water-insoluble PAG grease is primarily used in applications requiring excellent lubrication, stability, and resistance to water washout, often found in automotive and industrial sectors. Conversely, water-soluble PAG grease is designed for applications where ease of cleanup and lower toxicity is essential, commonly used in food processing and certain manufacturing environments. Both types leverage PAG’s superior lubricating properties for enhanced performance.</p></p>
