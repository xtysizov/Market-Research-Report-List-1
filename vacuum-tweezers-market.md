<p><p>The vacuum tweezers market can be categorized into two main types based on their mechanism of operation: with external mechanical pump and without external mechanical pump. Vacuum tweezers with external mechanical pumps utilize a separate pump to generate suction, allowing for precise handling of delicate components. In contrast, those without external pumps rely on built-in mechanisms for suction, making them more portable and easier to use. Both types serve various applications in electronics, automotive, and medical industries due to their efficiency in gripping and manipulating objects.</p></p>
