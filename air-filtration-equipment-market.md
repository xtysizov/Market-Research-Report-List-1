<p><p>The air filtration equipment market is categorized based on resistance levels measured in Pascals (Pa). Low-resistance filters (≤50 Pa) are suitable for general applications with minimal energy consumption. Medium-resistance filters (50-80 Pa, 80-120 Pa) handle moderate air quality control in commercial settings. High-resistance filters (120-220 Pa) serve industrial environments requiring robust filtration. Filters with resistance greater than 220 Pa are designed for specialized applications, such as cleanrooms and high-purity environments, demanding superior filtration efficiency and filtration media.</p></p>
