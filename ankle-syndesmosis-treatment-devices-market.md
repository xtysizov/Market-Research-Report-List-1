<p><p>The Ankle Syndesmosis Treatment Devices Market primarily includes two main types: Suture Button Fixation and Screw Fixation. Suture Button Fixation utilizes a button-like device that secures the fibula and tibia through soft tissue, allowing for controlled motion and early rehabilitation. In contrast, Screw Fixation involves inserting a screw to stabilize the syndesmosis, providing rigid immobilization. Both methods aim to restore stability and facilitate healing after syndesmosis injuries, catering to varying patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
