<p><p>The sanding and abrasive accessories market is categorized into three main types: bonded, coated, and others. Bonded abrasives consist of abrasive grains firmly attached to a backing material, providing durability for heavy grinding and cutting tasks. Coated abrasives feature abrasive grains attached to a flexible substrate, ideal for sanding and finishing applications. The 'others' category includes specialized products such as non-woven abrasives and synthetic fibers, used for niche applications in industrial and consumer settings. Together, these types address diverse surface preparation needs.</p></p>
