<p><p>The adjustable frequency drive market encompasses three primary types: AC drives, DC drives, and servo drives. AC drives regulate the speed and torque of AC motors by varying the frequency and voltage of their power supply, making them widely used in industrial applications. DC drives control the speed of DC motors through voltage adjustment, suitable for specific applications requiring precise control. Servo drives provide accurate position and speed control for servo motors, commonly used in robotics and automation, enhancing performance in dynamic environments.</p></p>
