<p><p>The waveform generator market can be categorized into several types based on the waveform produced. Square-wave generators output a series of high and low signals, ideal for digital circuits. Triangle generators create linear rise and fall patterns, used in modulation applications. Sawtooth generators produce ramp-like waveforms, commonly used in audio synthesis. Sine-wave generators offer smooth, continuous waveforms essential for analog applications. Arbitrary function generators can create complex waveforms as needed. Other types may include pulse generators and noise generators, serving specialized functions.</p></p>
