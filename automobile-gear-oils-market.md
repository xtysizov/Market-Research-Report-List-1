<p><p>The automobile gear oils market is categorized into three main types: mineral oil, synthetic oil, and semi-synthetic oil. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, provides basic lubrication and is cost-effective but may degrade faster under extreme conditions. Synthetic oil, engineered using chemical processes, offers superior performance, increased durability, and better temperature resistance. Semi-synthetic oil blends both mineral and synthetic oils, providing a balance of performance and cost, making it a popular choice for many automotive applications.</p></p>
