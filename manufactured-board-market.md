<p><strong>The Manufactured Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plywood</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manufactured board market primarily consists of Plywood and Oriented Strand Board (OSB). Plywood is made by gluing together thin layers of wood veneers, offering strength, versatility, and aesthetic appeal. It is widely used in furniture, construction, and flooring. OSB, on the other hand, is crafted from wood strands bonded with adhesives and pressed into sheets. Known for its durability and cost-effectiveness, OSB is commonly used in structural applications like sheathing and flooring. Both boards cater to diverse industrial and residential needs.</p></p>
