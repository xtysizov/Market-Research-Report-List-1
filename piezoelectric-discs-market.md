<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Discs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unimorph</li><li>Bimorph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric discs market is primarily categorized into unimorph and bimorph types. Unimorph piezoelectric discs consist of a single layer of piezoelectric material bonded to a passive substrate, allowing for bending in response to electric fields. Bimorph discs, on the other hand, are made of two layers of piezoelectric materials bonded together, enabling more significant bending and enhanced performance. Both types are widely used in actuators, sensors, and various applications in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics sectors.</p></p>
