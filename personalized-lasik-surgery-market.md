<p><p>The personalized LASIK surgery market includes several advanced techniques. Wavefront LASIK utilizes detailed eye mapping to tailor the procedure to individual visual imperfections. Topography-guided LASIK adds corneal surface mapping for precise corrections. Bladeless LASIK employs femtosecond lasers for more accurate tissue removal without a blade. Presby LASIK is designed for individuals with presbyopia, enhancing near and distance vision. Other offerings may include customized treatments or enhancements based on patient-specific needs and conditions, contributing to overall market diversity.</p></p>
