<p><p>The Portable Filtration System market includes three primary types: Pressure Filtration, Centrifugal Filtration, and Vacuum Filtration. Pressure Filtration utilizes pressure to separate solids from liquids, making it effective for high-throughput applications. Centrifugal Filtration employs centrifugal force to enhance separation efficiency, ideal for various industrial processes. Vacuum Filtration relies on a vacuum to draw liquids through a filter medium, suitable for low-viscosity liquids. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to diverse filtration needs across industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
