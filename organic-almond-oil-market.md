<p><p>The organic almond oil market comprises two primary types: sweet almond oil and bitter almond oil. Sweet almond oil is extracted from edible almonds and is commonly used in cosmetics, cooking, and skincare due to its moisturizing properties. In contrast, bitter almond oil, derived from non-edible almonds, contains compounds that can be toxic and is typically used in small quantities for aroma in perfumes or as a flavoring agent. Both types cater to distinct consumer needs and preferences in the natural oil market.</p></p>
