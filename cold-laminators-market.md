<p><p>The cold laminators market is divided into two main types: electric and manual cold laminators. Electric cold laminators are powered machines that automate the lamination process, offering efficiency and ease of use, ideal for high-volume applications. In contrast, manual cold laminators require manual operation, providing flexibility for smaller tasks and lower costs, making them suitable for home or small business use. Both types serve to protect and enhance documents or images without the use of heat, ensuring high-quality finishes.</p></p>
