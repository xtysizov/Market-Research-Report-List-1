<p><strong>The Hot-Air Coffee Roasters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Drum Roasters</li><li>Perforated Drum Roasters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot-air coffee roasters utilize different types of drum designs to achieve optimal roasting results. Solid drum roasters have a solid, non-perforated surface that retains heat effectively, promoting even roasting and rich flavor development. In contrast, perforated drum roasters feature holes in the drum that allow hot air to circulate through the beans more efficiently, enhancing control over the roasting process. Both types cater to various preferences, impacting flavor profiles and roasting times, appealing to both artisan and commercial coffee producers.</p></p>
