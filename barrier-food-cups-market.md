<p><p>Barrier food cups are categorized based on their material composition, each offering unique properties that enhance food preservation. Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) provides flexibility and resistance to impacts. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is known for its strength and moisture barrier. Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) offers excellent oxygen barrier properties, ideal for shelf-life extension. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is durable and versatile but less eco-friendly. Biaxial Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) is lightweight and provides a good moisture barrier. Polystyrene (PS) is often used for its rigidity and insulation. Other materials may include biodegradable options or composites that cater to specific packaging needs.</p></p>
