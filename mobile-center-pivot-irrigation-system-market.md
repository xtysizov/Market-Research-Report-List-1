<p><p>The Mobile Center Pivot Irrigation System market is segmented into various key components. Pivot Points facilitate the rotation of the system around a central point. Control Panels manage operations and settings for efficiency. Spans are the sections of the system that extend water delivery across fields. Sprinkler Drops distribute water from the mainline to the ground. Tower Drive Wheels allow for smooth movement across the field, while the Drive Train transmits power to these wheels, ensuring effective irrigation.</p></p>
