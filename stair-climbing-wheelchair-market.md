<p><p>The stair climbing wheelchair market comprises two main types: automatic and manual stair climbing wheelchairs. Automatic stair climbing wheelchairs utilize advanced technology, such as powered tracks or wheels, to navigate stairs effortlessly, providing users with greater independence and ease of use. In contrast, manual stair climbing wheelchairs require physical effort from a caregiver or the user, employing mechanisms like levers or rollers to ascend or descend stairs. Both types cater to individuals with mobility challenges, enhancing access to multi-level environments.</p></p>
