<p><p>The crystal fiber laser market is primarily categorized into Ruby Single Crystal Fiber Lasers and YAG (Yttrium Aluminum Garnet) Single Crystal Fiber Lasers. Ruby lasers are known for their high peak power and efficiency in applications such as industrial cutting and medical procedures, benefiting from their distinctive red wavelength. YAG lasers, on the other hand, offer superior thermal management and versatility, making them ideal for a broader range of applications, including materials processing and laser marking, due to their robustness and high output power.</p></p>
