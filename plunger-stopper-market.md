Chlorinated butyl rubber offers enhanced gas impermeability and superior chemical resistance, making it ideal for pharmaceutical applications where airtight seals are crucial. In contrast, bromobutyl rubber provides excellent thermal stability and is preferred for products requiring high elasticity and resilience. Both materials play vital roles in ensuring the integrity and safety of pharmaceutical and healthcare packaging, impacting performance and shelf life.</p></p>
