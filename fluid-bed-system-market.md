<p><p>A Fluid Bed System is a technology used in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, to process materials in a controlled manner. This system suspends solid particles in an upward-flowing stream of air or gas, creating a 'fluid-like' behavior that enhances mixing, heat transfer, and mass transfer. It is commonly employed for drying, granulation, and coating processes, offering advantages such as uniform product quality, reduced processing times, and enhanced energy efficiency.</p><p>The Fluid Bed System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.7% during the forecast period.
