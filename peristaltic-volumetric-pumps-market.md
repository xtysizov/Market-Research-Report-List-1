<p><p>Peristaltic volumetric pumps are a type of positive displacement pump that are designed to move fluids through a flexible tube via the action of a rotating roller or shoe. This mechanism allows for precise volumetric flow control, making them ideal for applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and wastewater treatment. The ability to handle a variety of fluids, including viscous and shear-sensitive materials, enhances their versatility.</p><p>The Peristaltic Volumetric Pumps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.50% during the forecast period. <p><p>The peristaltic volumetric pumps market features two primary types of speed regulation: mechanical and frequency speed regulation. Mechanical speed regulation utilizes gears or variable drive mechanisms to control the pump's output flow rate, ensuring consistent performance across varying conditions. In contrast, frequency speed regulation employs variable frequency drives (VFDs) to adjust the motor speed, allowing for precise control over the flow and pressure, enhancing energy efficiency and adaptability in various applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
