<p><strong>The Wireless Mouse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Wireless</li><li>Optical Wireless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless mouse market features two primary types: laser wireless and optical wireless mice. Laser wireless mice utilize a laser light source for tracking, providing enhanced precision on various surfaces, including glossy ones. They are ideal for detailed tasks and offer longer battery life. In contrast, optical wireless mice use LED light for detection, which works best on opaque surfaces. They are typically more affordable and suitable for everyday use. Both types benefit from the convenience of wireless connectivity.</p></p>
