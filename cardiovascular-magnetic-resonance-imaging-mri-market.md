<p><p>Cardiovascular Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) is a non-invasive imaging technique used to assess heart conditions and vascular diseases. The market is divided into two main types: Open MRI and Closed MRI. Open MRI features a wider opening and less confining design, making it more comfortable for patients, particularly those with claustrophobia. In contrast, Closed MRI systems offer higher-resolution images due to stronger magnetic fields, providing more detailed diagnostics, though they may be less comfortable for some patients.</p></p>
