<p><p>The liquid flow measuring instruments market includes several key types. Velocity flowmeters measure the speed of liquid to determine flow rate, while differential pressure flowmeters calculate flow based on pressure differences across a restriction. Volume flowmeters directly measure the amount of liquid passing through a point, and open channel flowmeters assess flow in channels with free surface flows. Additionally, the "Other" market segment encompasses specialized devices like mass flowmeters and Coriolis flowmeters, catering to specific industrial applications.</p></p>
