<p><strong>The Vegetated Green Roof Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extensive (Shallow Depth) Modules</li><li>Semi-Intensive (Intermediate Depth) Modules</li><li>Intensive (Maximum Depth) Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vegetated green roof market comprises three main types based on soil depth. Extensive modules feature shallow soil profiles, supporting drought-resistant plants and requiring minimal maintenance, making them cost-effective. Semi-intensive modules have an intermediate depth, allowing a wider variety of plants and moderate maintenance. Intensive modules, with maximum depth, support diverse plant life, including shrubs and small trees, necessitating higher maintenance and structural support. Each type caters to different aesthetic, ecological, and functional needs in urban environments.</p></p>
