<p><p>The photonic crystal fiber laser market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers provide a constant output of laser light, ideal for applications requiring steady power, such as materials processing and telecommunications. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers emit light in short bursts, which enhances precision and control, making them suitable for applications like medical procedures and micro-machining. Each type caters to distinct needs across various industrial sectors.</p></p>
