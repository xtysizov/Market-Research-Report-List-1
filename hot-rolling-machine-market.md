<p><strong>The Hot Rolling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rough Rolling Frame</li><li>Medium Rolling Rack</li><li>Finishing Rack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot rolling machine market comprises three main types: rough rolling frames, medium rolling racks, and finishing racks. Rough rolling frames are used for initial shaping and reducing the thickness of metal slabs or billets, preparing them for further processing. Medium rolling racks continue the shaping process, offering more precise dimensional control. Finishing racks provide the final adjustments, ensuring desired specifications and surface quality. Together, these types facilitate the effective production of various metal products in the manufacturing sector.</p></p>
