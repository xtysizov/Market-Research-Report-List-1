<p><strong>The Automotive Retread Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive retread tire market is primarily divided into two types: pre-cure and mold cure. Pre-cure retreading involves applying a pre-cured tread onto a used tire, ensuring uniformity and flexibility. This method offers quick production times and economical benefits. In contrast, mold cure retreading involves placing a tire into a mold where freshly compounded rubber is applied and cured simultaneously, creating a tire with superior performance and durability. Both methods extend the lifespan of tires while promoting sustainability.</p></p>
