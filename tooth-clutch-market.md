<p><p>The Tooth Clutch market consists of various types, primarily categorized into Electro Magnetic and Pneumatic types. Electro Magnetic Tooth Clutches use electromagnetic force to engage and disengage the clutch, providing precise control and quick response times, ideal for electric drives. In contrast, Pneumatic Tooth Clutches utilize compressed air to operate, offering robust performance in high-torque applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs, with Electro Magnetic suited for precision tasks and Pneumatic excelling in heavy-duty environments.</p></p>
