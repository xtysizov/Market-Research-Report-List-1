<p><p>TPEG, or thermoplastic elastomer blends, are categorized primarily into Paste Type and Flaky Type markets based on their physical forms and applications. Paste Type TPEG is characterized by its viscous consistency, making it ideal for applications requiring smooth, flexible materials such as sealants and adhesives. On the other hand, Flaky Type TPEG is produced in thin sheets and is often used in coatings and molds due to its easy handling and processing capabilities, catering to diverse industries like automotive and consumer goods.</p></p>
