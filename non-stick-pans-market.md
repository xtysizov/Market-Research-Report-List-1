<p><p>The non-stick pan market primarily features two types: PTFE-based and ceramic-based non-stick pans. PTFE-based pans utilize a synthetic polymer, known for excellent durability and easy food release, making them popular among consumers. In contrast, ceramic-based non-stick pans offer a more eco-friendly alternative, utilizing a silica-based coating that is free from harmful chemicals. While PTFE pans excel in heat resistance and longevity, ceramic pans appeal to health-conscious buyers seeking safer, natural cooking options.</p></p>
