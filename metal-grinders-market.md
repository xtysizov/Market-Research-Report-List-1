<p><p>The metal grinders market comprises various types, primarily including angle grinders and straight grinders. Angle grinders feature a rotating disc set at a right angle to the handle, ideal for cutting, grinding, and polishing materials at various angles. Straight grinders, on the other hand, have a straight shaft design, offering precision for detailed tasks like deburring and finishing in tight spaces. Both tools are essential in industrial, construction, and DIY applications, driving demand for efficient metalworking solutions.</p></p>
