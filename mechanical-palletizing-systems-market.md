<p><p>Mechanical palletizing systems can be categorized into low-level and high-level systems based on their design and functionality. Low-level palletizers involve stacking products at a lower height, making them suitable for smaller operations and reducing product damage. High-level palletizers, on the other hand, stack items at greater heights, optimizing space and efficiency in larger production environments. Both types enhance automation, improve safety, and increase throughput, catering to different operational needs in material handling and logistics.</p></p>
