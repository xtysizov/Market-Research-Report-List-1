<p><strong>The Back-contact Heterojunction Solar Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IBC Solar Cells</li><li>EWT Solar Cells</li><li>MWT Solar Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Back-contact heterojunction solar cells feature electrodes located on the rear side, enhancing efficiency by allowing maximum light absorption on the front. IBC (Interdigitated Back Contact) solar cells have both positive and negative contacts at the back, minimizing shading. EWT (Embedded Wafer Technology) solar cells integrate wafers into the cell design for improved performance and lower costs. MWT (Metal Wrap Through) solar cells utilize a unique design where the conductive pathways pass through the cell, enhancing efficiency and reducing material usage.</p></p>
