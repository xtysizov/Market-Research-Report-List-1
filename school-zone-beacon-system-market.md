<p><p>The School Zone Beacon System market can be categorized into two main types: Cloud-Based and Local Scheduler systems. Cloud-Based systems leverage internet connectivity to offer centralized control and management of beacons across multiple locations, enabling real-time updates and remote monitoring. In contrast, Local Scheduler systems operate independently, using on-site hardware to manage beacons based on pre-set schedules. Both types enhance school zone safety by alerting drivers to reduced speed limits, particularly during school hours, improving overall traffic management.</p></p>
