<p><p>The capacitive encoder market is divided into two primary types: linear encoders and rotary encoders. Linear encoders measure position along a straight path, utilizing capacitance changes as an object moves, providing high precision in applications like CNC machines and industrial automation. Rotary encoders, on the other hand, detect angular position and rotational movement, commonly used in robotics and motor control. Both types leverage capacitive sensing technology to offer reliable, accurate measurements across various industries.</p></p>
