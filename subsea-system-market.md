<p><ul><li>Subsea Production System</li><li>Subsea Processing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea system market comprises two primary types: subsea production systems and subsea processing systems. Subsea production systems involve equipment and technologies for extracting hydrocarbons from underwater reservoirs and transporting them to the surface. This includes subsea trees, flowlines, and manifolds. Conversely, subsea processing systems enhance hydrocarbon recovery by processing fluids directly on the seabed, which improves efficiency and reduces costs. These systems play a crucial role in enabling access to deep-water resources while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
