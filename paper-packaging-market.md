<p><p>The paper packaging market comprises three main types: paperboard, container board, and corrugated board. Paperboard is a thick paper used for packaging items like cosmetics and food due to its strength and printability. Container board is designed for making shipping containers, offering durability and resistance to environmental factors. Corrugated board, often used in boxes, consists of an inner fluted layer sandwiched between two flat liners, providing superior cushioning and protection for fragile goods during transport.</p></p>
