<p><p>Active Protection Systems (APS) enhance military vehicle survivability by intercepting incoming threats. The market is divided into two main types: hard-kill and soft-kill systems. Hard-kill APS physically intercepts and neutralizes projectiles using countermeasures like missiles or projectiles. In contrast, soft-kill APS employs electronic measures such as jamming, decoys, or smoke to mislead or evade incoming threats. Both types serve to improve vehicle defenses against emerging threats on the battlefield, with unique approaches tailored to specific combat scenarios.</p></p>
