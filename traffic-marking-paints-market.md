<p><p>The traffic marking paints market encompasses various types designed for road applications. Thermoplastic marking paint is durable and heat-activated, ideal for high-traffic areas. Water-based marking paint is environmentally friendly and easy to clean, suitable for less demanding applications. Solvent-based marking paint offers strong adhesion and resistance to weather but can release volatile organic compounds. Two-component road marking paint combines different components for enhanced performance and durability. Other types may include sprayable or low-temperature variants, catering to specific needs in traffic management and safety.</p></p>
