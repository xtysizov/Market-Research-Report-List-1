<p><p>The Signal & Data Cables market comprises three primary types: Twisted Pair, Coaxial, and Optical Fiber cables. Twisted Pair cables, made of copper, are widely used for telecommunications and networking due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Coaxial cables, also copper-based, offer higher bandwidth and are commonly used for cable television and internet services. Optical Fiber cables, made from glass or plastic fibers, provide unparalleled data transmission speed and distance, making them ideal for high-performance networking and telecommunication applications.</p></p>
