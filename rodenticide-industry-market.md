<p><strong>The Rodenticide Industry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anticoagulants Rodenticides</li><li>Non-anticoagulants Rodenticides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rodenticide industry is categorized into two main market types: anticoagulants and non-anticoagulants. Anticoagulant rodenticides disrupt blood clotting, leading to internal bleeding in rodents, and are widely used due to their effectiveness and ease of application. Non-anticoagulants encompass various active ingredients, including metal phosphides and neurotoxins, offering alternative mechanisms of action. These products often act more rapidly and can be useful in specific environments where anticoagulants may pose risks, such as around non-target wildlife.</p></p>
