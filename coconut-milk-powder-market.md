<p><p>The coconut milk powder market comprises two main types: pure coconut milk powder and mixed coconut milk powder. Pure coconut milk powder is derived solely from coconuts, offering a rich, authentic flavor and higher fat content, making it ideal for culinary uses and health products. In contrast, mixed coconut milk powder blends coconut with other ingredients, such as starches or milk powders, to enhance texture and reduce costs. This type is often used in processed foods and beverages for its versatility and affordability.</p></p>
