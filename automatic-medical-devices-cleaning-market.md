<p><p>The automatic medical devices cleaning market comprises two primary types of detergents: enzymatic and non-enzymatic. Enzymatic detergents utilize specific enzymes to break down organic contaminants, enhancing cleaning efficiency and reducing residue. These are particularly effective for removing proteins and biofilms from surgical instruments. In contrast, non-enzymatic detergents rely on surfactants and other chemicals to achieve cleanliness, typically requiring more mechanical action. Both types are essential for ensuring the safety and functionality of medical devices by adhering to stringent hygiene standards.</p></p>
