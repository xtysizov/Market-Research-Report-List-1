<p><p>The carton erector market is divided into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic systems. Semi-automatic carton erectors require manual intervention for certain steps, such as loading the flat cartons, but automate the folding and sealing processes, making them cost-effective for smaller operations. In contrast, automatic carton erectors handle the entire process autonomously, from carton loading to sealing, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs in high-volume production settings. These distinctions cater to varying operational needs and production scales.</p></p>
