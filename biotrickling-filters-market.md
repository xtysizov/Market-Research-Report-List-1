<p><p>Biotrickling filters are advanced biological treatment systems used for the removal of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and odors from industrial exhaust air. These systems enhance air quality by utilizing microorganisms to degrade harmful pollutants as air is passed through a layered media, promoting both efficient mass transfer and biofilm growth. Biotrickling filters operate optimally under varying conditions and provide a low-energy cost solution for air pollution control.</p><p>The Biotrickling Filters Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing environmental regulations, the rising awareness of air quality issues, and the expanding industrial sector.
