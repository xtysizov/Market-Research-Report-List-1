<p><strong>The Digital Differential Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicon Based</li><li>Foil Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Differential Pressure Sensors are classified into two main types: silicon-based and foil-based sensors. Silicon-based sensors leverage micromachined silicon elements to detect pressure differences with high sensitivity and accuracy, making them ideal for compact applications and environments prone to vibrations. Foil-based sensors, on the other hand, utilize thin metal foils to gauge differential pressure, offering robustness and reliability in harsh conditions. Both types are essential in various industries, including HVAC, automotive, and process control, due to their distinct advantages.</p></p>
