<p><strong>The RTV Silicone Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RTV-1</li><li>RTV-2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RTV silicone rubber market is categorized into RTV-1 and RTV-2 types. RTV-1, or one-part silicone, cures upon exposure to moisture in the air and is typically used for sealants and adhesives, offering convenience and ease of use. RTV-2, or two-part silicone, requires mixing a resin with a hardener before application, providing better control over curing times and properties. RTV-2 is often preferred for applications like molds and complex shapes due to its superior physical characteristics and customization options.</p></p>
