<p><p>Chemical peristaltic pumps are categorized into hydraulic, mechanical, and solenoid types, each serving specific operational needs. Hydraulic peristaltic pumps utilize fluid pressure to create movement, offering high flow rates and efficient handling of viscous fluids. Mechanical peristaltic pumps rely on rotating mechanisms to compress the tubing and facilitate fluid transfer, providing precise flow control. Solenoid peristaltic pumps utilize electromagnetic coils to create pulsations, allowing for accurate dosing and low-energy operation, making them suitable for laboratory and small-scale applications.</p></p>
