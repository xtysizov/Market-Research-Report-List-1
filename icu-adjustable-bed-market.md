<p><p>The ICU adjustable bed market comprises three main types: electric adjustable beds, semi-automatic adjustment beds, and manually adjustable beds. Electric adjustable beds feature motorized mechanisms for ease of position changes, enhancing patient comfort and care efficiency. Semi-automatic adjustment beds require minimal manual effort, often using assistive technologies for adjustments. Manually adjustable beds rely entirely on physical effort for positioning, offering a cost-effective option but with less convenience. Each type serves specific patient needs and caregiver requirements in healthcare settings.</p></p>
