<p><p>The PVC protective gloves market features two main types: powdered PVC gloves and powder-free PVC gloves. Powdered PVC gloves are coated with a light dusting of powder, enhancing ease of wear and removal, making them suitable for various applications, especially in medical settings. In contrast, powder-free PVC gloves eliminate the risk of powder-related contamination, which is vital in sterile environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including healthcare, food processing, and cleaning, emphasizing safety and hygiene.</p></p>
