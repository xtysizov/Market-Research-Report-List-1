<p><strong>The Automotive Paint Thickness Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Induction</li><li>Eddy Current</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Photothermal</li><li>THz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive paint thickness measurement market comprises several key technologies: </p><p>1. **Magnetic Induction**: Used for ferrous substrates, it measures paint thickness based on magnetic field changes.</p><p>2. **Eddy Current**: Used for non-ferrous metals, it detects coatings through induced currents.</p><p>3. **Ultrasonic**: Employs sound waves to assess thickness and can penetrate various substrates.</p><p>4. **Photothermal**: Uses thermal energy to measure thickness based on thermal response.</p><p>5. **Terahertz (THz)**: Utilizes terahertz radiation to analyze paint layers non-destructively. </p><p>Each method provides unique advantages depending on substrate materials and requirements.</p></p>
